# ROADMAP_CANON.md

**The Permanent Roadmap Namespace of AMALGAM**

Status: Canonical (namespace reservation only)
Nature: Mission-identifier reservation — not a design document, not an implementation guide

---

## Preamble

The sole purpose of this document is to reserve the mission identifiers from Mission 7.4
through Mission 10 and to permanently freeze mission numbering. It defines no objectives, no
deliverables, and no implementation, and it invents no features.

Every entry below is and remains **RESERVED**. Each reserved slot (architecture,
documentation, test plan, acceptance criteria) is intentionally empty; content is assigned to
a mission only later, when that mission is formally specified under the governance and
amendment process of PROJECT_CONSTITUTION.md.

Historical roadmaps assigned various and mutually conflicting meanings to several of these
numbers. Those proposals are preserved strictly as design history in ROADMAP_EVOLUTION.md and
FUTURE_PROPOSALS.md and are **not carried forward** into this namespace. Reserving a number
here confers no objective, no plan, and no feature.

The **Depends On** field records ordinal sequence only — the order in which the namespace is
frozen — and implies no content relationship between missions.

**Sources.** PROJECT_CONSTITUTION.md, CURRENT_ARCHITECTURE_STATE.md, RECOVERY_VALIDATION.md,
ROADMAP_EVOLUTION.md, FUTURE_PROPOSALS.md. The repository was not inspected and the
conversation was not reread.
